About Me

Hi! My name is Heather Seaman and I am a web development student. I have experience with both design and development and enjoy working with both sides of the process! I love helping small businesses express a mission and brand in unique and professional ways.

I have a working knowledge of basic front-end development, including Git, HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, JavaScript, and jQuery. Some of my design skills include competency with Adobe Photoshop, InDesign and Illustrator for both print and web design. You can find more about my projects on the portfolio page, and connect with me on the contact page. I look forward to hearing from you!
